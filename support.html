Support future SimpleKit tools

What support helps fund

  • Ongoing updates to existing planning tools
  • Better UX, clearer results, and stronger educational content
  • New SimpleKit calculators and guides over time
  • Hosting, maintenance, and platform improvements

Why the support ask is lightweight

SimpleKit is meant to feel useful first. Support is presented as an optional way to keep the project healthy without adding aggressive popups, fake urgency, or a hard paywall.
