About

SimpleKit is built to make planning feel lighter

The goal is simple: useful tools, plain-English guidance, and faster clarity for decisions that usually feel heavier than they need to.

What SimpleKit is

SimpleKit is a growing platform of practical planning tools for retirement, savings, net worth, account choices, and travel budgeting. The experience is designed to feel approachable, useful, and clear instead of bloated or overly technical.

What SimpleKit is not

SimpleKit is not a brokerage, an enterprise dashboard, or a substitute for professional advice. It is a browser-based toolkit meant to help you understand tradeoffs, organize numbers, and make better decisions faster.

Why the platform works this way

No login by default

Less friction makes it easier to start when you only need a quick answer.

Plain English first

The tools and guides are written to reduce intimidation, not add more jargon.

Connected tool paths

Each page suggests the next helpful tool or guide instead of leaving you at a dead end.

Practical outcomes

The focus is on decisions you can actually act on, not just more data for its own sake.
