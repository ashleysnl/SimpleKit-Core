What this tool is for

Net worth is often the simplest way to ground bigger planning decisions. It helps you understand what you own, what you owe, and whether your balance sheet is getting stronger over time.

Use it when you want to

  • Create a simple financial baseline
  • See how debt changes your overall position
  • Get context before using FIRE or retirement tools
