FIRE Explained

FIRE stands for financial independence, retire early. In practice, it is really about how much freedom your savings can buy and how sustainable that freedom needs to be.

Explore FIRE Check Net Worth First

What matters most

Your savings rate and your spending level usually matter more than trying to guess the perfect return assumption. The lower your spending target, the smaller the portfolio needed to support it.

What FIRE calculators help with

  • Turning a rough goal into a more realistic target
  • Comparing how different spending levels affect your timeline
  • Seeing when a traditional retirement plan may still be the better fit
