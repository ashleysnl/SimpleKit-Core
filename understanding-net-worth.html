Understanding Net Worth

Net worth is a snapshot, not a scorecard. It shows what you own, what you owe, and where you stand right now.

View Your Net Worth Use with FIRE Planning

What net worth includes

Net worth is assets minus liabilities. That can include cash, investments, home equity, and debts such as mortgages, loans, and credit balances.

Why it helps

  • It gives you a baseline before making bigger plans
  • It shows where debt is pulling against progress
  • It helps you measure change over time without guessing
